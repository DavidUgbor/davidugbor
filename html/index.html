— Bio

Based in Port Harcourt. Built for the field.

I was raised in Port Harcourt — Nigeria's oil capital — surrounded by rigs, refineries, and the language of hazard. I trained at Charkin Maritime Offshore Safety Center, completing STCW Basic Safety, Advanced Fire Fighting (IMO 2.03), OPITO HUET 5092 with EBS, and ISPON HSE Levels 1 & 2.

From 2022 to 2024 I worked at Fieldoption Engineering Ltd as a Fire Watch and Safety Officer — standing watch over live industrial operations, managing hot-work permits, conducting daily fixed-system checks, and running HSE compliance across active field sites.

The Advanced Fire Fighting cert is not a wall decoration. It means BA entry, smoke-filled compartment search, foam and CO₂ attack, casualty removal. The fire watch is the prevention. The AFF is the answer if prevention fails. Both belong on the same officer.

Off the water, I invest in the technical side of the industry. Certified in the Git & GitHub Bootcamp (Colt Steele) and Linux Shell Scripting (Jason Cannon) — the same command-line discipline that runs vessels also runs the infrastructure behind them.
